# Abuse Escalation Playbook

### Overview
This playbook defines the process for handling abuse, phishing, or malware reports.

### Steps
1. Collect all relevant evidence (logs, URLs, screenshots).
2. Tag the ticket as `escalate_to_abuse_team`.
3. Notify the Abuse Team via internal Slack channel or ticket system.
4. Document actions taken and timestamp in the case management tool.

### Escalation Rules
- All phishing/malware cases must be reviewed within 24 hours.
- Abuse Team will decide whether to suspend the domain or request further evidence.
