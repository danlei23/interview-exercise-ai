## Section 4 â€“ Domain Suspension and Reactivation

### 4.1 Scope
This policy applies to all registered domains managed by our platform.

### 4.2 Suspension Reasons
A domain may be suspended if:
- The WHOIS contact information is missing or invalid;
- There are unresolved policy violations such as abuse, phishing, or malware;
- Billing has not been paid within the grace period.

### 4.3 Reactivation
To reactivate a suspended domain:
1. Correct any WHOIS issues.
2. Resolve any policy or billing violations.
3. Contact the support team with evidence that the issue is fixed.
