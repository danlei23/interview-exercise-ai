# Billing FAQ

### Q1: What happens if I miss an invoice payment?
If an invoice is not paid within 30 days of issue, services such as domain hosting may be suspended.

### Q2: How can I reactivate my service?
Once payment is confirmed, services will be automatically restored within 24 hours.

### Q3: Can I request an invoice extension?
Yes, please contact the Billing Support Team before the due date to request a payment extension.
