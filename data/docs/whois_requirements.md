# WHOIS Requirements

### Overview
All domain registrants are required to provide accurate and up-to-date WHOIS contact details.

### Required Fields
1. Registrant Name (organization or individual)
2. Email address
3. Telephone number
4. Physical address (street, city, postal code, country)

### Policy Enforcement
- Domains with missing or incorrect WHOIS data will be temporarily suspended.
- Registrars must notify the registrant within 5 business days to correct the information.
- Failure to update the WHOIS record within 15 days may result in permanent suspension.
